Chapter 1

a) What is your Definition of experience?

My definition of experience is what I have done in my life, how I remembered this experience, and how it could help me in the future.

b) Include two or three key experiences and how they have shaped you

One time that changed me a lot was in 2009, when I got bullied by the other kids in Preschool, and then stood up for myself in preschool.

This changed me, because it made me find out that I have to stand up for myself when things start getting tough in life.

Another time that changed me was in 2013 when I learnt how to do multiplication and division.

This changed me, because it made me find out that I have a great sense of logical thinking and I can do much better if I am able to put my mind around things.

The last time that has changed me was when the COVID-19 pandemic hit, and I learnt new things.

This changed me signifigantly, because it taught me how fragile the world is and how there are different ways to learn at home, thus opening my mind more to myself.

c) Based on your experiences, what career would you apply for?

I would be a Financial Analyst.

d) Explain why you would pick this job?

I picked this job, because a financial analyst has to go through lots of tough times like me in preschool,

does a lot of difficult mathmatical calculations like me learning in 2013, and finally should understand how the world and markets are affected like me during the Pandemic.

This is why I have chose to become a financial analyst for one of my future careers.
